Hey sweetheart,

When I sit down to recollect the 4 years we have spent together I am unable to describe them or you or us in the right words. I just know you were the first friend I made on campus and I know you are the one who has always been the closest even when you weren’t. for you people kept coming as going but for me it was only you. if there was something I wanted to say or express I only had you, I don’t think I ever told anyone else among our group. Many times without saying you know what is wrong with me, well this at times makes me realize how much special you are. I can not write much or express my love or feelings but I guess by now you know I am always there whenever you need me or even if I am not there I do understand you. I don’t remember if we ever had a fight or anything till date. We just mutually understand things of each other and agar kuch cheez is going to turn up into a fight we both know it and stop it there.

You are so loving caring and expressive that I genuinely want to do so so much for you but somehow fall short everytime. I can never match up to the amount you care and love. You love selflessly without thinking. You put yourself into a person if u really find him or her important. I don’t know how important I am to you but you are really important to me. Waise I know thoda to special I am . We will and have all got in busy in our lives and this will keep increasing our misunderstandings and other things but I wish for us to be like guys in this and just not bother about it all and have a gala time.

There are so many things we have done in the four years which I will cherish, many of those u sent me in a mail you wrote some time back. The photos u mail of our old times is so damn touching kabhi kabhi. College days were surely the best days we could have had. I wish to be with you again in Mumbai and have a lot of fun. Sorry for all the times I have hurt you. well I don’t have much to write, keep enjoying the way u do baby. I love you always. Happy birthday! :*
